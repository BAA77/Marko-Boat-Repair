Boat Engine Care Tips

Expert Advice from Marko Boat Repair - Your Local Marine Repair Experts

Experiencing boat engine issues or mechanical woes? Marko Boat Repair's expert advice offers timely solutions, expert diagnostics, and quality repairs in Split, Croatia

#1

Regular Maintenance

Stay afloat with confidence! Routine maintenance safeguards your boat's engine, reduces downtime, and saves you money

  • Check oil and fluid levels regularly
  • Replace filters every 50-100 hours
  • Inspect belt and hoses for wear
#2

Performance Optimization

Unleash your boat's potential! Performance optimization boosts speed, efficiency, and agility for an unparalleled yachting experience

  • Tune engines annually
  • Clean propellers and shafts regularly
  • Monitor fuel consumption

#3

Troubleshooting

Minor issue today, major repair tomorrow! Troubleshooting prevents escalating problems and keeps your boat running smoothly

  • Identify unusual noises or vibrations
  • Check for leaks or corrosion
  • Monitor engine temperature
#4

Seasonal Operation

Stay seaworthy year-round! Seasonal boat maintenance prevents damage, reduces repairs, and maintains peak performance

  • Winterize engines before storage
  • Prepare engines for launch season
  • Check safety equipment
